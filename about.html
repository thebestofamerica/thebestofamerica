Who We Are

A Permanent Record of American Character

The Best of America™ is an independent national archive dedicated to honoring the individuals, institutions, and communities that represent the highest ideals of American life. We believe that every generation produces extraordinary people whose stories deserve to be preserved — not just for today, but in perpetuity.

Founded during America's 250th anniversary year, the archive was created to ensure that the quiet acts of courage, service, and sacrifice that define this nation are never forgotten. From veterans and first responders to community leaders and historic institutions, these are the stories that textbooks often miss but that communities carry in their hearts.

What We Believe

Built on Three Principles

Permanence

Every record in this archive is preserved permanently. These stories will endure as long as this nation does.

Accessibility

This archive belongs to the American people. Access is free, open, and unrestricted — always.

Independence

We are not affiliated with any government entity, political party, or commercial interest. Our only allegiance is to the truth of these stories.

The Founding 250

Honoring America's 250th Year

In recognition of the nation's semiquincentennial, The Best of America Archive has launched the Founding 250 — an ongoing initiative to identify and permanently record individuals and institutions whose stories exemplify the best of what this country has produced over two and a half centuries. These founding records represent every branch of service, every region of the country, and every form of sacrifice that has kept this republic intact.

Nominations remain open. If you know an American whose story deserves to be preserved, we invite you to submit their nomination below for consideration.
