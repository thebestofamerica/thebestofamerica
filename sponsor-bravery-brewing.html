Their Story

United by Craft, Rooted in the Antelope Valley

Bravery Brewing Co. was founded in 2011 at a time when the Antelope Valley had no craft breweries to call its own. From the very beginning, the team behind Bravery set out to do more than brew great beer — they wanted to create a gathering place where new friendships could be forged, stories could be told, and lasting memories could be made. Their Grand Opening fell on Independence Day, 2012, a date that perfectly captures the spirit with which this brewery was built.

Starting on a modest 3-barrel brewhouse with just 20 barrels of fermentation capacity, Bravery's founders embraced their small scale as a creative advantage — constantly exploring new beer styles and refining their core lineup. By 2016, demand had outpaced what their original system could handle, and they expanded into what is now a 15-barrel production facility, complete with an award-winning barrel-aging program that has earned the brewery recognition well beyond the Antelope Valley.

Today, Bravery Brewing operates a full taproom and pizza kitchen at 42705 8th Street West in Lancaster. The space is warm, welcoming, and unmistakably American — exactly what its founders intended.

★  The Wall of Bravery

The moment you step inside Bravery's taproom, you cannot miss the Wall of Bravery — a massive American flag adorning the southern wall, framed by hundreds of photographs of service members and pieces of military memorabilia brought in by patrons over the years. It is a living tribute, growing with every customer who walks through the door and adds a face to the wall. At Bravery Brewing, honoring those who served isn't a slogan. It's built into the architecture of the place itself.

Recognition

Why Bravery Brewing Earns the Patriot Business Designation

The Local Patriot Business designation at TheBestofAmerica.org is reserved for businesses that go beyond commerce — companies that actively strengthen the communities they serve and demonstrate a genuine commitment to American values. Bravery Brewing Co. earns this distinction on every count.

As a veteran-owned independent brewery, Bravery was built from the ground up with a mission to serve — the community, the craft, and the legacy of those who wore the uniform. Their Wall of Bravery is one of the most extraordinary grassroots military memorials found in any small business in Southern California. Their community involvement is deep and consistent, and their doors have been open on Independence Day — the day they first opened — every year since 2012.

Bravery Brewing also carries the Brewers Association Independent Craft Seal, a mark of integrity in an industry increasingly dominated by corporate ownership. They are independently owned, locally operated, and proudly rooted in the Antelope Valley.
