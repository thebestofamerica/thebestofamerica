Local Patriot Businesses

American businesses that support our mission to preserve and honor the stories of those who have served, sacrificed, and strengthened this nation.

Our Partners

Businesses That Stand With Us

Local Patriot Businesses are companies across America that have pledged their support to The Best of America Archive. They believe, as we do, that preserving the stories of our nation's heroes is not just a civic duty — it is an investment in the character of future generations.

Construction & Trades
Hollis & Sons Builders
Fort Bragg, North Carolina

Family-owned since 1978. Specializing in residential construction and restoration, with a longstanding commitment to hiring veterans transitioning to civilian careers.

Bravery Brewing Co.
Lancaster, California · Est. 2012

Founded by veterans and rooted in the Antelope Valley since 2012, Bravery Brewing is more than a craft brewery — it's a community gathering place that honors those who served. Their Wall of Bravery displays hundreds of photos of service members, making every pint a tribute.

Food & Hospitality
Liberty Bell Diner
Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

A neighborhood institution since 1962. Known for their Veterans Eat Free program every Sunday and their unwavering support of local first responder families.

Legal Services
Whitehorse & Associates
Albuquerque, New Mexico

Pro bono legal services for veterans and military families. Over 2,000 cases handled at no cost since the firm's founding in 2004.

Retail & General Store
Cornerstone General Store
Springfield, Illinois

Operating continuously since 1897. A community gathering place and proud sponsor of local youth programs, scholarship funds, and civic events.

Healthcare
Pacific Coast Physical Therapy
San Diego, California

Veteran-owned practice providing rehabilitation services with a focus on returning service members. Partnered with three VA hospitals in the region.

Agriculture
Dupree Family Farms
Baton Rouge, Louisiana

Five generations of Louisiana farming. Donates produce to local food banks and employs transitioning veterans through their Harvest Heroes program.

Join the Network

Become a Local Patriot Business

If your business shares our commitment to honoring American service and character, we invite you to join the Local Patriot Business network. A one-time sponsorship of $750 grants your business a place in the archive in perpetuity. This sponsorship is our sole source of income and what allows the archive to exist. It is not a commercial transaction — it is a statement of values and a lasting investment in preserving the stories that matter most.

Patriot Businesses receive recognition in the archive, a digital seal for their website and storefront, and the knowledge that they are helping preserve the stories that matter most.
